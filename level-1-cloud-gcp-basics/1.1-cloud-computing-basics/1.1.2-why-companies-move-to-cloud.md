# 1.1.2 Why companies move to cloud

---

## 1. Concept Explanation (Simple English)

Companies move to the **cloud** because:

> **Cloud makes IT faster, cheaper, and easier to manage.**

Earlier, companies had to:
- Buy servers
- Maintain hardware
- Hire infrastructure teams
- Plan capacity years in advance

Cloud removes most of this burden.

---

## 2. Main Problems with Traditional (On-Premise) Systems

Before cloud, companies faced:

- High upfront cost (servers, data centers)
- Slow setup (months)
- Difficult scaling
- Hardware failures
- Maintenance headaches
- Limited global reach

These problems slowed business growth.

---

## 4. Reason #2 ‚Äî Easy Scaling (Very Important)

In cloud:

- Streaming apps during events

Traditional systems:
- Cannot scale quickly
- Often crash under load

Cloud handles growth smoothly.

---

## 5. Reason #3 ‚Äî Speed and Agility

Cloud allows companies to:
- Launch systems in minutes
- Test ideas quickly
- Deploy updates faster

This helps companies:
- Innovate faster
- Beat competitors
- Respond to market changes

Speed = competitive advantage.

---

## 6. Reason #4 ‚Äî Reliability and Availability

Cloud providers offer:

- High availability
- Disaster recovery

If one server fails:
- Another takes over automatically

Traditional systems:
- Often have single points of failure

Cloud reduces downtime significantly.

- Support 24/7 operations

Global business becomes easier.
