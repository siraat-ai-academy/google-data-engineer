# 0.1.4 Why companies collect data

---

## 1. Concept Explanation

Companies collect data because:

> **Data helps companies understand what is happening in their business.**

Without data:
- Companies guess
- Decisions are risky
- Money is wasted

With data:
- Decisions are based on facts
- Problems are visible
- Growth becomes possible

Data helps companies move from:
> “I think this is happening”  
to  
> “I know this is happening”

---

## 2. Main Reasons Companies Collect Data

Companies collect data to:

- Understand customers
- Improve products and services
- Increase profit
- Reduce cost
- Predict future trends
- Stay ahead of competitors

Data is the **foundation of modern business decisions**.

---

## 3. Customer Understanding (Very Important)

Companies collect data to know:

- Who are their customers?
- What do customers like?
- What do customers dislike?
- How customers use products?

### Example:
An online app collects:
- Clicks
- Time spent
- Search history

This data helps:
- Improve user experience
- Recommend better content
- Increase engagement

---

## 4. Improving Products and Services

Data helps companies answer:

- Which feature is used most?
- Which feature is ignored?
- Where users face problems?

### Example:
A food delivery app collects:
- Order time
- Delivery time
- Ratings

This data helps:
- Improve delivery speed
- Remove bad restaurants
- Improve service quality

---

## 5. Increasing Revenue (Making More Money)

Companies use data to:

- Identify best-selling products
- Run targeted marketing
- Set better prices

### Example:
Retail companies collect:
- Sales data
- Customer purchase history

They use this to:
- Offer discounts
- Recommend products
- Increase sales

---

## 6. Reducing Cost and Waste

Data helps companies find:

- Where money is wasted
- Which processes are inefficient

### Example:
A factory collects:
- Machine usage data
- Energy consumption data

This helps:
- Reduce power usage
- Prevent machine failures
- Save money

---

## 7. Risk Management and Fraud Detection

Companies collect data to:

- Detect fraud
- Reduce risk
- Improve security

### Example:
Banks collect:
- Transaction data
- Location data
- Login activity

They use this to:
- Detect suspicious activity
- Block fraud transactions
- Protect customers

---

## 8. Predicting the Future (Advanced Use)

Companies use historical data to:

- Forecast sales
- Predict demand
- Plan inventory

### Example:
E-commerce companies predict:
- Festival demand
- Seasonal trends

This helps:
- Avoid stock shortages
- Avoid overstock
- Plan ahead

---

## 9. Decision Making (Most Important Reason)

At the end:

Data helps companies make decisions such as:
- Hire more staff or not
- Launch new product or not
- Expand business or not

Good data → good decisions  
Bad data → bad decisions  

That is why data quality is critical.

---

## 10. Engineer Mindset (Why This Matters for You)

As a Data Engineer, your job is to:
- Collect data correctly
- Store data safely
- Make data usable
- Ensure data is reliable

If data is wrong:
- Reports are wrong
- Decisions fail
- Trust is lost

Your work directly impacts business success.

---

## Key Takeaway

**Companies collect data to understand, improve, decide, and grow.**

Data is not collected for fun.
It is collected to **run the business better**.
